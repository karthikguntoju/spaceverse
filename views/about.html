About Spaceverse

Spaceverse is an educational, interactive 3D experience built to inspire curiosity about our Solar System. We combine accurate planetary models, approachable explanations, and engaging quizzes to help learners of all ages explore space in a memorable way.

Our Vision

We envision a world where learning about space is playful and accessible. Spaceverse aims to bridge the gap between scientific accuracy and delightful interaction — enabling anyone to explore the cosmos from their browser.

Mission

Build an accessible, delightful web application that teaches core planetary science concepts through immersive 3D visuals and interactive activities.
